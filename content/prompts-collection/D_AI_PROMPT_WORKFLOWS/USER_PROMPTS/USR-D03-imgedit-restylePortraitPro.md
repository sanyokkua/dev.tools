USR-D03-imgedit-restylePortraitPro

# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing

# Description
Single-shot image-editing prompt that re-renders a portrait as a modern professional/cinematic camera shot, preserving identity while allowing lighting/look to change. Attach the source portrait.

# Prompt
Re-render the portrait in this image as if captured with a modern professional/cinematic camera and lens. Keep the person's identity, facial structure, expression, hairstyle, and pose recognizable and unchanged; you MAY restyle the lighting, color grade, depth of field, and overall photographic look to a polished, cinematic quality (flattering key light, soft falloff, pleasing bokeh, filmic color). Keep it photorealistic and natural — no beautification that alters identity, no reshaping of the face or body. This is a creative re-style of the photographic treatment, not a change of who the person is.

(Attach the source portrait. Restyle — identity locked, photographic look may change.)

# Parameters
None — fixed-content, single-shot prompt. Input is the attached image.

# Example Values
N/A — attach a portrait to re-grade as a pro/cinematic shot.
