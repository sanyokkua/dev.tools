# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (GPT Image 2 / 1.5)

# Description
Raise the technical quality of an undamaged portrait — sharpness, clarity, detail, lighting, and color — without changing the person, pose, or composition. Use this when you have an intact but low-quality portrait (blurry, noisy, compressed, hazy, dim, or flat) and you want a clean, high-quality version that still looks like the same real photo.

# Prompt
This is a real, undamaged portrait photo — likely a casual smartphone or compressed image — that simply needs higher quality. Do not treat this as damaged; just improve the image quality. Remove blur, motion blur, focus softness, JPEG compression artifacts, sensor noise, grain, haze, and color fringing or chromatic aberration. Increase real sharpness and micro-contrast on the eyes, eyelashes, eyebrows, lips, hair strands, and skin so fine detail is crisp. Recover detail and dynamic range in any too-dark or too-bright areas, and improve the lighting realism, white balance, and color accuracy for natural, true-to-life tones. Render natural skin texture with visible pores and fine wrinkles — do not smooth, airbrush, or plastic-ify the skin, and do not change facial proportions. Keep the facial features exactly as they appear in the uploaded image — same eyes, nose, mouth, and face shape — and keep the same expression, pose, head angle, hairstyle, makeup, clothing, background, framing, and composition unchanged. Do not beautify, restyle, add or remove anything. Output a photorealistic, high-resolution photograph at the same aspect ratio as the uploaded image, with natural colors, realistic skin tones, and clean, professional-camera clarity.
