# Description
Revive an old black-and-white photo in one pass — repair damage, add natural color, and bring it to modern flagship-camera quality, keeping the scene and people exactly the same. Use when you have an old, damaged, faded, or black-and-white photograph and want a single prompt that restores, colorizes, and modernizes it at once.

# Prompt
Edit the provided photograph. Treat it as the source and keep it the same photograph — do not regenerate, reinterpret, replace, or re-stage the scene, and do not invent new people, objects, or setting. In one pass, apply only the three families of edits described below — damage repair, era-appropriate colorization, and quality modernization — as a careful non-destructive revival, and keep every real element pixel-faithful to the source image. The result should look like the same scene, with the same people, photographed today on a top-tier camera.

Tasks:
- restore physical condition: remove scratches, tears, creases, fold lines, dust, spots, stains, water marks, mold, grain, and noise
- reconstruct missing or worn-away detail seamlessly, matching surrounding texture and lighting
- reverse aging: lift fading, clear haze and yellowing, recover detail lost in the brightest highlights and deepest shadows
- add natural, realistic, era-appropriate color: believable healthy skin tones, natural hair and eye color, lifelike lips
- color clothing, fabrics, accessories, furniture, walls, plants, sky, and every surface in coherent, period-plausible hues
- keep colors grounded and convincing rather than vivid or stylized
- modernize quality: crisp natural sharpness, recovered fine texture, accurate white balance, pleasing realistic lighting, wide dynamic range, clean gentle HDR-like tonal depth
- keep material realism in skin, fabric, metal, wood, glass, and foliage; keep skin texture natural with real pores
- keep the same people with their exact identities, faces, facial structure, features, expressions, and ages
- keep the same poses, body proportions, hairstyles, clothing design, objects, background, scene layout, composition, camera angle, and framing

Output style: ultra-clean modern flagship-camera quality; realistic optics; natural era-appropriate color; realistic skin tones; subtle HDR; high clarity; photorealistic; detailed but natural.

Negative constraints: do not regenerate or restage the scene; do not change identity, facial structure, features, expression, or age; do not change pose, body proportions, hairstyle, or clothing design; do not add, remove, move, restyle, or invent anyone or anything; do not change the background, scene layout, composition, camera angle, or framing; no plastic, waxy, or AI-looking skin; no overprocessing; no excessive sharpening; no over-saturation; no neon or anachronistic colors; no stylization; no cartoon look; no warped anatomy; no extra fingers; no distorted eyes; only repair the damage, add the color, and lift the quality, and keep everything else unchanged.
