# Description
Re-render a casual portrait as a modern high-end professional / editorial camera shot while keeping the exact same face and identity. Use this when you have a plain snapshot of a person and want it to look like a polished studio or editorial headshot — soft key light, clean backdrop, 85mm shallow depth of field — without changing who the person is.

# Prompt
This is a casual, everyday portrait photo of a person. Re-render it as a modern, high-end professional portrait that looks like it was captured with a full-frame mirrorless camera and an 85mm f/1.8 lens. Keep the facial features exactly as they appear in the uploaded image — same eyes, nose, mouth, and face shape — and keep the same person, identity, expression, head angle, hairstyle, and overall pose. You may upgrade the lighting and look: add soft, flattering studio key light with a gentle fill and subtle rim light, a clean and slightly out-of-focus professional backdrop, and a natural shallow depth of field with smooth, creamy background blur (bokeh). Keep skin texture realistic and natural with visible pores and fine detail — no plastic smoothing, no beauty-filter look — and keep realistic, true-to-life skin tones. Refine clarity, micro-contrast, dynamic range, and color so the result is crisp and editorial. Do not change the person's identity, facial structure, age, body shape, or clothing; do not add accessories or alter the face. Output a photorealistic, high-resolution professional portrait at the same aspect ratio as the uploaded image, with cinematic, high-end-camera quality and natural color.
