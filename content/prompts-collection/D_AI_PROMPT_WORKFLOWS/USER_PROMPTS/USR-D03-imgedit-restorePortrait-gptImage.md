# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (GPT Image 2 / 1.5)

# Description
Repair physical damage on an old or damaged portrait photo while keeping the person's identity, expression, and framing exactly as they are. Use this when you have a scanned or aged portrait with scratches, tears, creases, dust, stains, fading, or heavy noise and you want a clean, natural modern photographic restoration — not a restyle or beauty edit.

# Prompt
This is an old, damaged portrait photograph that has been scanned. Carefully restore it to look like a clean, well-preserved photograph. Repair all physical damage: remove scratches, tears, rips, creases, fold lines, dust specks, spots, stains, water marks, mold, and film grain or scan noise; fix faded and discolored areas and rebuild any missing or torn parts of the image by reconstructing the surrounding detail realistically. Recover lost fine detail in the skin, eyes, hair, eyebrows, eyelashes, and clothing so the portrait reads as a sharp, natural photograph. Gently rebalance the contrast, exposure, and tonal range so the image is even and lifelike, with realistic shadows and highlights. Keep the facial features exactly as they appear in the uploaded image — same eyes, nose, mouth, and face shape — and keep the same expression, pose, head angle, hairstyle, clothing, background, and composition. Do not beautify, slim, smooth, restyle, age, or change the person in any way; do not move or crop anything. Keep natural skin texture with visible pores and fine lines — no plastic or airbrushed look. Output a photorealistic, modern-quality photograph at the same aspect ratio as the uploaded image, with natural colors and realistic skin tones.
