# Prompt ID

# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (JoyAI Image Edit)

# Description
Revive an old black-and-white photo in one pass — repair damage, add natural color, and bring it to modern flagship-camera quality, keeping the scene and people exactly the same. Use when you have an old, damaged, faded, or black-and-white photograph and want a single prompt that restores, colorizes, and modernizes it at once.

# Prompt
Take this old, damaged photograph and bring it fully back to life in a single pass, as if the very same moment were being photographed today on a top-tier modern camera. Keep the warm, authentic feeling of the original scene, but make it look clean, vivid, and alive.

First, repair every sign of age and damage. Carefully remove scratches, tears, cracks, creases, dust, spots, stains, water marks, and fading, and gently rebuild any detail that has been lost or torn away. Clean away film grain, noise, blur, and haze.

Then add natural, realistic, era-appropriate color to this black-and-white and faded image. Give the people believable, healthy skin tones, dress them in period-correct clothing colors, and let the background and environment take on natural, coherent colors that feel true to the time and place.

Finally, modernize the technical quality. Recover sharp fine detail and realistic textures in faces, hair, skin, eyes, and fabric, keeping skin looking natural and never plastic. Improve the lighting, dynamic range, local contrast, white balance, and color accuracy so the photo has rich, clean depth and looks crisp and professional.

Throughout all of this, keep everything real exactly the same. Do not change the people or their identities, faces, expressions, ages, poses, or hairstyles. Keep the same clothing design, the same objects, the same background, the same scene layout, composition, camera angle, and framing. Do not add, remove, or rearrange anything — only repair it, color it, and upgrade its quality. The final result should be photorealistic, with natural colors and realistic skin tones.
