# Prompt ID

# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (JoyAI Image Edit)

# Description
Re-render a casual portrait as a modern high-end professional camera shot while keeping the exact face and identity. Use when you have a snapshot or casual portrait you want to look like a studio/editorial headshot, without changing who the person is.

# Prompt
Re-render the portrait in this image as a modern, high-end professional camera photograph while keeping the person's face and identity exactly the same. Transform the look into a polished studio or editorial headshot: soft, flattering key light with gentle fill and a subtle catchlight in the eyes, a clean and uncluttered backdrop, and a shallow depth of field with smooth background blur (bokeh) as if shot on a full-frame camera with an 85mm f/1.8 lens. Render crisp, natural detail in the eyes, hair, and clothing, with realistic skin texture and natural skin tones — no plastic smoothing, no heavy makeup. Apply professional, color-accurate grading with balanced contrast and pleasing tonal depth. Keep the person's exact facial features, identity, bone structure, expression, gaze, age, hairstyle, and pose unchanged — only the lighting, background, focus, and overall photographic quality should look professional. The result should be indistinguishable from a high-end DSLR or mirrorless portrait of the same person. Keep the subject recognizable and unchanged.
