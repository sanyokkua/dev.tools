# Description
Transform a real photo into an anime/cartoon illustration while keeping the same subject(s), pose, and composition recognizable. Use when you have a real photograph and want a stylized anime or cartoon version of it.

# Prompt
Edit the provided photograph by converting it into an anime / cartoon illustration. Keep the same people, poses, expressions, clothing, key features, framing, and overall composition as the source — anyone who knows the original should immediately recognize it as the same scene rendered in an illustrated style. Only change the rendering medium from photographic to drawn, as described below; do not re-stage the scene, change who is in it, or rearrange the layout.

Tasks:
- re-draw in a clean, appealing anime / cartoon aesthetic with smooth line art
- use cel-style shading with soft gradients
- give expressive, stylized eyes and features while keeping each person recognizable
- render skin and hair as flattering illustration rather than photographic detail
- use vibrant, harmonious colors
- stylize the background to match the same illustrated look while keeping its layout and main elements in the same places
- keep proportions natural and the characters recognizable
- keep the same subjects, poses, expressions, clothing, framing, and composition as the source

Output style: polished anime / cartoon illustration; clean line art; cel shading with soft gradients; vibrant harmonious colors; appealing and consistent.

Negative constraints: do not rearrange the scene or change the composition; do not change the pose, expression, or framing; do not change who or what is in the image; do not add or remove people or objects; do not change clothing design or color scheme; keep proportions natural; no warped anatomy; no extra fingers; no distorted eyes; keep the identity, pose, layout, and story of the image the same while the medium becomes illustration.
