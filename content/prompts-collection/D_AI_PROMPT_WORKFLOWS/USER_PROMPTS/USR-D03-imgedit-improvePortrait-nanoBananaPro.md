# Description
Raise the quality of a portrait — sharpness, detail, clarity, color — without changing identity, pose, or composition. Use when you have an undamaged but soft, noisy, hazy, or low-quality portrait that just needs to look better.

# Prompt
Edit the provided photograph. Treat it as the source and keep it the same photograph — do not regenerate, reinterpret, replace, or re-stage the scene, and do not invent a new person or setting. Assume there is no physical damage to repair; this is purely a non-destructive quality upgrade. Apply only the quality improvements described below and keep everything else pixel-faithful to the source image.

Tasks:
- remove blur, motion softness, JPEG and compression artifacts, sensor noise, color noise, haze, chromatic aberration
- increase real sharpness and micro-contrast where it matters most — eyes, eyelashes, eyebrows, skin pores, lips, individual strands of hair
- recover fine detail and texture in skin, hair, and fabric
- restore dynamic range so shadows and highlights both hold detail
- keep skin texture natural and realistic with visible pores and fine detail
- improve lighting realism, white balance, and color accuracy so skin tones look true and natural
- create clean, subtle HDR-like tonal depth while remaining photorealistic
- keep the same person, identity, expression, pose, camera angle, framing, and composition exactly as in the source

Output style: ultra-clean modern flagship-camera quality; realistic optics; natural colors; realistic skin tones; subtle HDR; high clarity; photorealistic; detailed but natural.

Negative constraints: do not regenerate or restage the scene; do not change identity; do not change facial structure or features; do not change expression; do not change pose, body shape, or proportions; do not change hairstyle, clothing, background, or composition; do not beautify, slim, retouch, or restyle anyone; no plastic, waxy, or airbrushed skin; no AI-looking skin; no overprocessing; no excessive sharpening; no fake bokeh; no cartoon look; no stylization; no warped anatomy; no extra fingers; no distorted eyes; keep everything else unchanged.
