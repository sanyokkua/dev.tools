# Description
Re-render a casual portrait as a modern high-end / professional camera headshot while keeping the exact face and identity. Use when you have an everyday phone snapshot of a person and want a studio / editorial-quality portrait look.

# Prompt
Edit the provided photograph. Keep the same person, exact face, identity, pose, head angle, expression, hairstyle, and the overall framing and composition as the source — do not regenerate a different person or re-stage the shot. Only change the rendering: lighting, optics, and photographic quality, as described below. The person must stay instantly recognizable as the same individual; everything about WHO this is and HOW they are posed is preserved, and only the photographic look changes.

Tasks:
- relight with a soft, flattering key light and gentle fill that wraps naturally around the face
- add clean catchlights in the eyes and smooth, believable shadow falloff
- render with the look of a premium full-frame camera and an 85mm f/1.8 portrait lens
- create a shallow depth of field: face tack-sharp, background softly and creamily blurred, with natural lens compression and realistic optics
- place the subject against a clean, simple, uncluttered professional backdrop
- keep skin texture natural and realistic with visible pores and fine detail; crisp eyes, eyebrows, and hair
- use accurate, natural skin tones, balanced color, and subtle photorealistic tonal depth
- keep the exact same face, identity, pose, head angle, expression, and framing as the source

Output style: polished modern studio / editorial portrait; high-end full-frame camera and 85mm look; soft key light; clean backdrop; shallow depth of field; realistic skin tones; photorealistic; detailed but natural.

Negative constraints: do not change identity; do not change facial structure, features, age, or expression; do not change the pose, head angle, or framing; do not beautify, slim, or reshape features; do not swap the person for someone else; no plastic, waxy, or airbrushed skin; no AI-looking skin; no overprocessing; no distorted eyes; no warped anatomy; no extra fingers; keep the person and pose unchanged while the lighting, lens look, and backdrop are upgraded.
