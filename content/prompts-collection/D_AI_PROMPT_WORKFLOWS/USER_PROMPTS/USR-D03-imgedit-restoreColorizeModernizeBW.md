# Prompt ID
USR-D03-imgedit-restoreColorizeModernizeBW

# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing

# Description
Single-shot image-editing prompt that restores physical damage, adds era-appropriate natural color, and modernizes technical quality in one pass — turning an old, damaged, or black-and-white photograph into a clean, photorealistic image that looks like the same scene shot today on a modern camera.

# Prompt
Restore, colorize, and modernize this old photograph in a single pass. Keep every person, face, identity, expression, age, pose, body proportion, hairstyle, clothing design, object, background, scene layout, composition, camera angle, and framing exactly as in the source — do not add, remove, rearrange, or reinvent anything.

Apply these three families of edits only:

1. Repair physical damage: remove scratches, tears, cracks, creases, fold lines, dust, spots, stains, water marks, and fading; reconstruct any missing or worn-away detail seamlessly using surrounding texture and lighting; remove film grain, noise, blur, haze, and compression artifacts; recover detail lost in blown highlights and crushed shadows.

2. Colorize: add natural, realistic, era-appropriate color across the whole image — believable healthy skin tones, natural hair and eye color, period-correct clothing colors, coherent colors for fabrics, accessories, furniture, walls, plants, sky, and every surface. Keep colors grounded and plausible for the time period; avoid oversaturation, color bleeding, and anachronistic hues.

3. Modernize quality: recover crisp natural sharpness and fine textures (skin pores, fabric weave, hair strands, detailed eyes); improve white balance, dynamic range, local contrast, and lighting realism; add subtle HDR-like tonal depth; maintain faithful material realism in skin, fabric, metal, wood, glass, and foliage. Keep skin texture natural — no plastic, waxy, or overprocessed look.

The result should read as the exact same scene, naturally restored and colorized, looking as if photographed today on a top-tier modern camera.

(Attach the source image. This is a three-step revival: damage repair, colorization, and quality modernization.)

# Parameters
None — fixed-content, single-shot prompt. Input is the attached image (no text parameters).

# Example Values
N/A — attach an old, damaged, or black-and-white photograph.
