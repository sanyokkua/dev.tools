# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (Nano Banana Pro (Gemini 3 Pro Image))

# Description
Apply a professional cinematic camera look and color grade to a nature or city scene while keeping all content and composition the same. Use when you have a flat or ordinary landscape/cityscape and want a high-end, film-like cinematic look.

# Prompt
Edit the provided photograph. Keep the same scene, the same landscape or cityscape, every building, object, and element, and the exact composition, framing, and perspective as the source — do not regenerate, re-stage, or invent a different scene. Only change the rendering: the photographic quality, mood, and color grade, as described below. The content and layout are preserved; only the cinematic look changes.

Tasks:
- render with a premium cinema-camera look: rich depth, natural optical falloff, a gentle widescreen feel, clean realistic detail
- apply a polished cinematic color grade — a warm golden-hour glow OR a refined teal-and-orange grade
- use deep but detailed shadows, controlled highlights, and a filmic tonal curve that feels expensive and atmospheric
- add a very subtle, fine film grain for texture
- keep contrast and saturation tasteful rather than overdone
- enhance natural atmosphere — soft light, gentle haze, or glow — only where it fits the existing scene
- keep all content, geometry, objects, buildings, and the composition exactly as in the source

Output style: high-end cinematic, professionally graded photograph; filmic tonal curve; teal-orange or golden-hour grade; subtle film grain; atmospheric; photorealistic and believable.

Negative constraints: do not regenerate or restage the scene; do not add, remove, move, or replace any objects, buildings, or elements; do not change the geometry, perspective, or composition; do not change the basic structure of the time of day; do not over-saturate or over-contrast; no surreal or fantasy elements; no stylization beyond a realistic film grade; keep everything else unchanged.
