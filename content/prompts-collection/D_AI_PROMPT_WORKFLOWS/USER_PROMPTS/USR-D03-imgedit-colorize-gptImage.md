# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (GPT Image 2 / 1.5)

# Description
Add natural, realistic, era-appropriate color to a black-and-white photo while keeping faces, composition, and all detail identical. Use this when you have a grayscale or sepia photo (portrait, group, landscape, or city) and want a believable color version without altering anything in the image.

# Prompt
This is a black-and-white (or sepia) photograph. Add realistic, natural color to it as if it had originally been photographed in color, without changing any of the content. Apply believable, era-appropriate colors throughout: natural and accurate skin tones, realistic hair and eye colors, plausible clothing colors suited to the period and setting, and natural colors for sky, foliage, water, wood, metal, stone, and architecture. Keep the coloring coherent and physically consistent with the lighting and shadows already in the photo. Do not change, add, remove, sharpen away, or move any detail — keep the exact same composition, framing, people, objects, textures, expressions, and grain structure. Keep the facial features exactly as they appear in the uploaded image — same eyes, nose, mouth, and face shape — for every person. Preserve the original tonal range and detail; only introduce color. Output a photorealistic, naturally colorized photograph at the same aspect ratio as the uploaded image, with subtle, realistic colors rather than over-saturated or artificial tones.

# Parameters
None — fixed-content, single-shot prompt. Input is the attached image (no text parameters).
