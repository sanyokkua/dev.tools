# Description
Improve a low-quality portrait's clarity and detail while keeping identity and pose unchanged. Use when you have an undamaged but blurry, noisy, hazy, or compressed portrait that needs higher quality.

# Prompt
Enhance the portrait of the person in this image to crisp, high-quality modern photograph quality, removing blur, motion blur, compression artifacts, sensor noise, haze, and chromatic aberration, increasing sharpness and micro-contrast on the eyes, skin, and hair, and recovering fine detail and dynamic range. Keep natural realistic skin texture with no plastic smoothing, and improve lighting, white balance, and color accuracy. Preserve the exact same facial features, expression, pose, framing, and identity.
