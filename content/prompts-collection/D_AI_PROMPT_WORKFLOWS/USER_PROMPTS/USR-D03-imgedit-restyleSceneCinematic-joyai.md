# Prompt ID
USR-D03-imgedit-restyleSceneCinematic-joyai

# Domain / Category
D — AI & Prompt Workflows / D03 Image Editing (JoyAI Image Edit)

# Description
Give a landscape or cityscape a professional cinematic camera look and color grade while keeping content and composition exactly the same. Use when you have a flat or ordinary scene you want to look like a high-end cinematic frame, without altering what is in the shot.

# Prompt
Re-render the landscape or cityscape in this image with a professional, cinematic camera look while keeping the content and composition exactly the same. Apply a high-end cinematic color grade — a refined teal-and-orange palette or a warm golden-hour glow — with rich but natural contrast, deep balanced shadows, and gentle film-like highlight rolloff. Add subtle, fine film grain and a slight, tasteful atmospheric depth for a premium motion-picture feel. Render the scene with the clarity and tonal depth of a high-end cinema camera and lens: crisp detail in foliage, architecture, and surfaces, smooth tonal gradients in the sky, and a wide, controlled dynamic range. Keep every structure, natural element, horizon line, perspective, and the overall framing and composition exactly where they are — do not add, remove, or move anything in the scene. The result should look like a polished cinematic still of the same place, dramatic yet believable and photorealistic. Keep the scene and composition unchanged.

# Parameters
None — fixed-content, single-shot prompt. Input is the attached image (no text parameters).
