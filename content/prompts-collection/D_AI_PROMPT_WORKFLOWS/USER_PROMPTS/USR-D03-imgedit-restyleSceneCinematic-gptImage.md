# Description
Apply a professional cinematic camera look and color grade to a landscape or cityscape while keeping all content and composition exactly the same. Use this when you have a flat or ordinary nature or city photo and want it to look like a cinematic film still — rich color grade, anamorphic feel, subtle grain — without changing the scene itself.

# Prompt
This is an ordinary outdoor photo — a landscape or city scene. Re-render it as a cinematic film still that looks like it was captured with a high-end cinema camera and anamorphic lenses. Keep the scene exactly the same — same composition, framing, perspective, horizon, buildings, objects, people, and layout — and do not add, remove, or move anything. Apply a professional cinematic color grade: rich, moody contrast with a teal-and-orange palette or a warm golden-hour tone, deep but detailed shadows, controlled highlights, and atmospheric depth. Add a gentle anamorphic widescreen feel, a soft cinematic glow on the brightest areas, and subtle, fine film grain for a filmic texture — keep it tasteful, not heavy. Improve clarity, dynamic range, and material detail in architecture, foliage, water, and sky while keeping everything photorealistic and believable. Do not change the actual content, time of day beyond grading, season, or geometry of the scene. Output a photorealistic, high-resolution cinematic image at the same aspect ratio as the uploaded image (or a subtle widescreen crop only if it does not cut important detail), with a polished, high-end-camera color grade.
