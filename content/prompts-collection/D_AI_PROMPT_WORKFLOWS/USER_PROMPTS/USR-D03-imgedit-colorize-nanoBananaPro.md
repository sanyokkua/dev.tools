# Description
Add natural, realistic, era-appropriate color to a black-and-white photo while keeping faces, composition, and all detail identical. Use when you have a grayscale or sepia photo and want a believable, naturally colorized version.

# Prompt
Edit the provided photograph. Treat it as the source and keep it the same photograph — do not regenerate, reinterpret, replace, or re-stage the scene, and do not invent new people, objects, or detail. This is a colorization only: apply only the addition of color described below as a non-destructive edit, and keep every face, identity, expression, all people and objects, the detail, textures, tonal values, lighting, and the exact composition and framing pixel-faithful to the source. Do not repair, sharpen, restyle, or alter the image in any way other than adding color.

Tasks:
- add natural, lifelike skin tones with subtle variation and healthy warmth
- add realistic hair, eye, and lip colors
- add plausible, period-appropriate colors for clothing, fabrics, and accessories
- add natural greens for foliage, realistic sky and water tones, and true-to-life colors for wood, stone, metal, and other materials
- match all colors to the apparent era and setting of the photo
- keep colors consistent across the whole image, with smooth, accurate color where edges and details meet
- preserve the original tonal values, shadows, highlights, lighting, and mood exactly
- keep all faces, people, objects, detail, and the composition exactly as in the source

Output style: photorealistic, natural, restrained color; era-appropriate palette; lifelike skin tones; consistent and convincing; the same photo, now in color.

Negative constraints: do not regenerate or restage the scene; do not change identity, faces, or expressions; do not change detail, textures, or composition; do not repair damage, sharpen, denoise, or restyle; do not alter the original tonal values, shadows, highlights, or lighting; no over-saturation; no artificial or neon colors; no anachronistic colors; keep everything else unchanged and only add color.
